Study Programmes

Courses

B.Sc Computer Science course is already running from the session 2005-2006 with intake of 100 seats. From session 2009-2010 intake is 200 seats.
Admission Criteria of B.Sc Computer Science Course
  • Admission on the basis of the result of Entrance test for B. Sc Computer Science conducted by the Punjab University in the month of August 2025.
  • Admissions are made on the inter-se merit of Entrance Test / Marks obtained in Graduation through notification of Govt. of Pb.
  • Fee structure according to Punjab Govt. / University norms.
Reservation Policy

Reservation of seats in various categories is as per the policy of Govt. of Punjab.
General Instructions
  • 15% seats are open to the candidates on All India basis, 85% seats are reserved for resident candidates of Punjab State.
  • The candidates on All India basis shall be eligible only for reservation provided by the constitution, i.e. 25% for SCs/STs and 5% for BCs.
  • Candidates from universities other than Punjab University will have to submit the Migration Certificate in original and original Detailed Marks Card within 30 days from the date of enrolment, failing which they will have to pay Rs. 1000/- as late fee to Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar.
