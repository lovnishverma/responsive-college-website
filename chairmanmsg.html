Message from Executive Director

IT education plays a vital role in the modernization and development of a nation. With the rapid advancement of technology, the demand for skilled IT professionals has increased exponentially. At NIELIT Chandigarh, we are committed to providing quality education and training in the field of Information Technology, Electronics, and Communication to meet this growing demand. Our institution strives to empower students with industry-relevant skills and hands-on experience, enabling them to contribute effectively to the digital economy. We focus on bridging the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements by offering a wide range of courses, including cutting-edge technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Big Data, IoT, and Cybersecurity. We take pride in nurturing talent and innovation while promoting a culture of continuous learning. Our faculty members, with their extensive experience and expertise, ensure that students receive the best education and guidance to succeed in their careers. I encourage students and professionals to make the most of the opportunities provided by NIELIT Chandigarh and become part of this journey toward building a stronger, technologically advanced nation. Together, we can shape a brighter future.

Sh. Deepak Wasan
Executive Director
